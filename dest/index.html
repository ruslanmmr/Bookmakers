Important Deciding Factors for Bookmaker's Rank in The List

Betting odds

This is a crucial factor when it comes to the comparison of bookmakers. High and attractive odds translate to huge winnings. As such, the higher the odds of a sportsbook, the higher the number of punters that will sign-up with it. This means that it will rank higher as compared to those that offer lower odds.

Broad betting range

Even when a large number of punters frequently bet on football, there is still the need for a bookmaker to have a broader range of betting selections. It serves no interest for a sportsbook to only offer betting for the 1st premier league when the best odds to bet on are those on Champions League matches. The more extensive a bookmaker is the higher will be its ranking.

Bet limit amount

When it comes to betting, punters don't like it when they are restricted on the upper limit of a bet, especially when there is a potential to win big. However, this is what some reputable sportsbooks offer. They do this by setting an upper and a lower betting limit amount.

Convenient deposit and withdrawal methods

Reputable online bookmakers provide all available options of depositing or withdrawing cash to allow gamblers to make deposits conveniently. For instance, some offer instant credit card transfer or used payment options such as Giropay, Click2Pay, or Paysafecard.

High welcome bonus

A wagering welcome bonus allows a new punter to quickly get familiar with the interesting sports betting arena. Experienced gamblers with over ten recommended top providers can gladly take an extra bonus code for either new or existing clients and then utilize them as extra capital on their wagering plans.

Which online bookmaker is the best? Player's guide

Which online bookmaker is the best? Most people ask this question, especially when they find the many types of established bookmakers in the market. In this section, we show you the factors to consider if you want to find the best bookmaker to invest in.

Licensing authority

The best online bookmaker is licensed by a legal gaming authority. A licensed bookmaker implies that the company is regularly checked and monitored and will always allow you to withdraw your money. The best online bookmaker, therefore, must have licenses from recognized and legal licensing commissions.

Security of money transactions

All money transactions done should be secure and ought to be conducted in a safe way. All the personal monetary details must remain as private as possible. Whenever requested for a new password, the identification process ought to be clear and secure.

Betting odds

Betting odds offered by the best online maker should in most cases exceed the 95 percent limit on particular matches. As such, when trying to get the right match to stake on, the odds should only experience minimum changes. Moreover, opinionated lines exist to prove there is a compiling service for every odd offered and it is not just copied from another online bookmaker or influenced by the market activity.

Variety of bets and markets

The best online bookmaker should at least feature more than 20 different sports from which a punter can choose his or her favorite bets. After all, how many people will sign up with a hockey betting site that doesn't offer basketball, tennis, or football? Moreover, an online bookmaker should be able to get a hundred betting markets in particular matches.

Live betting and live stream

This is a crucial factor that is synonymous with top-rated bookmakers. This is because some bettors prefer staking on sports events that are in progress. This is because they have time to assess the games situation. Live betting options on the best sites often come with longer or shorter odds that are determined by how the sporting event is unfolding.

User-friendly

The best online bookmaker must be easy to navigate when staking and searching for games to bet on. It must also be prompt when placing a bet and must issue your balance instantly. It should also be able to keep the history of all your transactions.

Customer service

Customer service is very important in the betting industry. The best online bookmaker should provide a variety of support systems to help players reach out to the customer desk with ease. The customer service team should also be able to respond to any question as soon as possible and in a satisfactory manner.
